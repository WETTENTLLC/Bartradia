Our Mission

"Trade Treasures, Save Together" - At Bartradia, we're passionate about group economics, global consciousness, and eco-friendly solutions. Our platform is dedicated to saving waste and unwanted items by giving them new life through community trading.

As an extension of WETT ENTERTAINMENT LLC, we've created a space where sustainability meets community, and where every item finds its true value.

What We Do

  1. 1

    Post Unwanted Items

    Simply take pictures and upload your items. Someone out there will find value in what you no longer need.

  2. 2

    Barter and Trade

    Engage in our badge and credit systems, a new form of currency for trading unwanted items.

  3. 3

    Global Impact

    Reduce waste and contribute to global sustainability by rehoming items that might have otherwise ended up in landfills.

Why It Matters

Eco-Friendly Solutions

Every item repurposed is one less item in a landfill. We're committed to reducing waste and promoting sustainable consumption.

Global Consciousness

Empowering people to help one another creates a ripple effect of positive change. Together, we can make a difference.

Supported Communities

Addressing needs across various demographics ensures no one is left behind. We believe in inclusive trading for all.

Our Vision

By fostering an environment of resourcefulness and community support, we aim to make the world a better place—one traded treasure at a time.

We envision a future where bartering and trading old belongings becomes second nature and where anyone, regardless of their circumstances, can find value and support through our platform.

Our goal is to create a circular economy where items continually find new purpose, reducing waste and building stronger communities in the process.

Join Us

Be a part of the change. Whether you're moving, decluttering, or simply looking to make a positive impact, Bartradia is here to help you turn unwanted items into opportunities.

As the saying goes, "One Man's Trash is Another Man's Treasure." Join us in this movement and let's save together!
