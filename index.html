How Our Credit System Works

Users have the opportunity to earn credits through various activities on our platform:

  • Trading Items: When users trade items of differing values, the difference is credited to the user with the higher-valued item. These credits can be used for future trades to balance out value differences.
  • Unlocking Premium Features: Use earned credits to access exclusive features and events.
  • Community Participation: Earn credits by participating in special activities and events, and by referring new users. More credits are earned when referred users complete their first trade, engage in high-value trades, or refer others, creating a recurring credit-earning cycle.
  • Tracking Credits: Credits can be tracked in real-time through your account dashboard.
