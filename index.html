How Our Credit System Works

Users have the opportunity to earn credits through various activities on our platform:

  • Trading Items: When users trade items of differing values, the difference is credited to the user with the higher-valued item.
  • Unlocking Premium Features: Use earned credits to access exclusive features and events.
  • Community Participation: Earn credits by participating in special activities and events, and by referring new users.
  • Tracking Credits: Credits can be tracked in real-time through your account dashboard.
